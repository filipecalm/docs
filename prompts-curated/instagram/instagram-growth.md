# Instagram Growth Kit

**Uso:** análise de conta, estratégia de crescimento, roteiros de Reels, posicionamento de marca e monetização.  
**Quando usar:** para melhorar performance orgânica no Instagram com estrutura de prompts prontos.

## Prompts

### 1. Instagram Growth Manager Mode
```
Act as a world-class Instagram strategist. My niche is [NICHE], my audience is [AUDIENCE], and my goal is [GOAL]. First conduct a complete growth audit and identify the biggest bottlenecks preventing growth. Then create a complete Instagram growth system covering content, positioning, engagement, profile optimization, growth strategy, and monetization opportunities.
```

### 2. Instagram Audit Mode
```
Act as an elite Instagram consultant. Analyze my Instagram account: Bio: [BIO] Niche: [NICHE] Audience: [AUDIENCE] Content Style: [CONTENT] Identify weaknesses, missed opportunities, profile optimization improvements, content gaps, engagement issues, and growth opportunities. Then create a prioritized action plan.
```

### 3. Reel Growth Machine
```
Act as a viral Instagram Reel strategist. For the topic [TOPIC], generate 20 reel concepts including hook, script structure, retention triggers, curiosity gaps, visuals, and CTA. Optimize every reel for watch time, shares, saves, and follows.
```

### 4. Personal Brand Builder
```
Act as a personal branding consultant. Analyze my niche [NICHE], strengths [STRENGTHS], goals [GOALS], and audience [AUDIENCE]. Create a positioning strategy that makes me memorable, trusted, differentiated, and recognizable within my niche.
```

### 5. The 90-Day Instagram Growth Plan
```
Act as a senior growth strategist. My niche is [NICHE]. My goal is [GOAL]. Create a complete 90-day Instagram growth roadmap including content pillars, posting frequency, engagement strategy, profile optimization, growth experiments, content series, and performance metrics.
```

### 6. Instagram Monetization Mode
```
Act as a social media business strategist. Analyze my niche [NICHE], audience [AUDIENCE], and current content strategy. Identify the fastest and most sustainable ways to monetize my Instagram through products, services, affiliates, sponsorships, consulting, memberships, digital products, or other relevant opportunities.
```
